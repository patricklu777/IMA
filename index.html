Violet Evergarden
Violet is a former soldier who was enlisted in the Leidenschaftlich army and fought in the war, where she was treated as nothing more than a weapon
Back when she was a child, Violet was found on a deserted island alone after Dietfried Bougainvillea and his comrades' ship became stranded.

synopsis

Violet's Garden of Eternity is an anime with a strong anti-war message that aired in January 2018. The story is about a young girl (Violet) who gradually comes to know her feelings from a killing machine.
The story progresses step by step, from memories of the war at the beginning, to writing letters as a "doll", to experiencing various things later on, and finally understanding human feelings.
The plot is somewhat similar to that of a unit play, with one story per sentence, each time a story between Violet and a different person, from a colleague to a person
who asks to write for her, from a humble soldier to a noble royal princess, she meets people with different identities and experiences different feelings.
The final words end not with Violet and XX (a different character), but with Violet and Love.
